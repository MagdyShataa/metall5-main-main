About us

DC provides fabrication, welding, mechanical and hydraulic services, and our work includes repair services, as well as custom manufacturing, or manufacturing of new components. We are able to deal with large industrial institutions and companies that need our various services, given our experience of more than 25 years, as we provide a variety of industries in projects ranging from simple to complex. We always strive to do our best to maintain the continuity of our relationship with our success partners, by understanding the nature of the services required and specifying them accurately to work on repairing them as quickly and with high quality.

Capital workshop

Our heavy equipment shop specializes in rebuilding, modifying and repairing heavy equipment (construction equipment, rock crushers, railway maintenance equipment, winch drums, Pettibone cranes, etc.). Our services are provided for all types of small and large machines, including horizontal lathes, vertical lathes (VTL), and horizontal boring mills (HBM). We also repair components of factory production machines (choppers, cutting machines, presses, punching presses, injection molding, compression presses, air locks, inlet/outlet vanes, vacuum pumps, gearboxes, hydraulics, etc.). We have a large-scale machine shop to manufacture spare parts according to need. We also manufacture new products according to customer demand, which include centrifugal pump housings, piston pumps, components for air conveying systems, cooling tower piping, and frame welding, to name a few.

Capital workshop

The capital complex, is an engineering workshop as well as an automobile workshop for lathes, automobile spare parts, hydraulic and diesel fuel injection pumps.

The Capital Company was established in 1990 AD, where it began in the field of manufacturing and automatic turning, by taking into account the correct indicators of the development path in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Taking into account the market demand, the organization was established to provide all industrial equipment and parts related to maintenance workshops, manufacturing, etc.
